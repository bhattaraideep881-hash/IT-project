About this website

Trekking Adventures is a student project that helps beginners discover and compare trekking routes.

Purpose

The website presents useful trekking information in a clear, readable and responsive layout. Visitors can browse photographs, compare routes and save their own ideas.

How to use it

1. Browse

Visit the Routes page and compare the difficulty, duration and elevation.

2. Add an idea

Use the simple form to add your own route to the table.

3. Prepare safely

Research the weather, equipment and local guidance before beginning any trek.

Basic trekking tips

Tip Why it matters
Train gradually Build fitness before a long trek.
Pack lightly Carry the essentials without unnecessary weight.
Stay hydrated Drink water regularly, especially at altitude.
Respect nature Take rubbish away and stay on marked paths.
